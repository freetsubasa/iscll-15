The 15th International Symposium on Chinese Languages and Linguistics (IsCLL-15)

Ever since 1990, the biennial International Symposium on Chinese Languages and Linguistics (IsCLL) has become a major platform for researchers working on Chinese linguistics to exchange their ideas and findings. The 15th International Symposium on Chinese Languages and Linguistics (IsCLL-15) will be held at National Hsinchu University of Education on May 27-29, 2016. With the main theme “Linguistic Theory and Application”, the conference hopes to create a dialogue between linguistic theory and its practice. Abstracts that address the main theme are particularly welcome, though abstracts on theoretical and descriptive studies on Chinese linguistics are cordially invited as well.

Theme: Linguistic Theory and Application

Date: May 27-29, 2016

enue: International Conference Room, National Hsinchu University of Education
