The 15th International Symposium on Chinese Languages and Linguistics (IsCLL-15)
May 27-29, 2016
CALL FOR PAPERS

The 15th International Symposium on Chinese Languages and Linguistics (IsCLL-15) will be held at National Hsinchu University of Education on May 27-29, 2016. With the main theme “Linguistic Theory and Application”, the conference hopes to create a dialogue between linguistic theory and its practice. Abstracts that address the main theme are particularly welcome, though abstracts on theoretical and descriptive studies on Chinese linguistics are cordially invited as well.

Important Dates:
  • Submission Deadline: Oct. 31, 2015
  • Notification: Jan 11, 2016
  • Final Paper Submission: March 31, 2016
  • Conference Dates: May 27-29, 2016
Invited Speakers: (Listed alphabetically)
  • Adele Goldberg (Psychology Department, Princeton University)
  • Chu-Ren Huang (Department of Chinese and Bilingual Studies, the Hong Kong Polytechnic University)
  • Randy LaPolla (Division of Linguistics and Multilingual Studies, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore)
