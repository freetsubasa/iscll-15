Submission

Submission guidelines:

  • Please submit your abstract via EasyChair Conference System.
  • Each person may submit no more than one single/first-authored abstract in addition to one joint-authored abstract.
  • Abstracts should not exceed 4 pages in length (references and data may be appended to the abstract).
  • Abstracts must be anonymous.
  • Authors should present their own papers at the conference.
  • For each accepted abstract, submission of a full paper is required.
  • Registration fee will be waived for one author of each accepted paper.
